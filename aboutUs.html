Who We Are

The Stanford Robotics Club is a student run robotics and mechatronics club on campus. Our purpose is to serve as a safe and fun worksapce for Stanford undergraduate and graduate students to pursue their interests in robotics.

What We Do

The goals of the Stanford Robotics Club are threefold: Engineering Projects, Annual Competitions, and Outreach.

Engineering Projects

Engineering projects are meant to encourage members to design and build robotic and mechatronic systems individually or in small teams and allow them to explore their interest in much greater depth.

Annual Competitions

Annual competitions allow members to develop a variety of skills in engaging team based engineering competitions.
