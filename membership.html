The Stanford Robotics Club is a do-ocracy!

Membership

You will join a vibrant community of learners, tinkerers, builders, and doers.

Eligibility

Membership is open to all registered Stanford students, including both undergraduate and graduate students--regardless of skill level.

Benefits

All members of the club enjoy the following benefits:

  • Free Club Shirt
  • Exclusive grants to fund projects
  • Access to dedicated workshops
  • Opportunities to give Tech Talks
  • Tours of tech companies and robotics startups
  • Monthly socials with professors and industry sponsors
  • Awesome listings of jobs and internships
  • Annual retreat

Membership Responsibilities

  • Respecting the Robotics Club's and members' property
  • Attending General Body meetings
  • Participating in mandatory club activities(e.g. Volunteering at FIRST LEGO League, Robot Block Party)

How To Join

  1. Pay club dues. The following options are available:
    1. $20 for full year
    2. $10 for one quarter
    3. $10 to renew
  2. Subscribe to our Mailing List.
  3. Join a Team or Start a Project.
  4. Volunteer at our Outreach Events.

Questions?

If you have any questions about membership, please email us at stanfordroboclub@gmail.com.
